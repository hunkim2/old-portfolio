After attending the University of Illinois: Urbana-Champaign, I fell into the rabbit hole known as programming. My curiousity and my drive to learn more about this ocean of knowledge, led me to LearningFuze, where I began my journey to become a front-end developer.

I've had the priviledge of working with some amazing like-minded developers. Passionate and filled with the urge to learn more and accelerate our knowledge, I delved deep into creating a web application that I not only had a hefty knowledge about, but had a long history with. Super Smash Stats was fruit of my hard-work and constant pursuit of programming. I led a team of three that eventually became the front-end of the project; including the UX-UI, dynamically generated data, and manipulation of back-end calls.

I crave to share my passion and my knowledge with my next team. Feel free to message or call me.
